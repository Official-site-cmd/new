ABOUT US

Your Perfect Getaway Awaits!

Just 500 meters from the historic Rustaveli Avenue, CNK Hotel invites you to stay at the crossroads of culture, comfort, and timeless Georgian style. Designed to reflect the warm character of Old Tbilisi architecture, the hotel’s charming exterior blends seamlessly into the historic part of the city. Inside, discover 25 double rooms, each offering a tranquil escape with modern amenities — air-conditioning, flat-screen satellite TVs, and cozy seating areas — ideal for unwinding after a day of discovery. But the crown jewel of CNK Hotel is high above the city - Cloud Restaurant — perched on the rooftop — offers more than a meal. It’s an experience. Savor the bold flavors of Georgian-Italian fusion cuisine, surrounded by panoramic views of Tbilisi's skyline, from the red-tiled rooftops to the distant Caucasus Mountains. Whether you're enjoying a romantic dinner or a sunset cocktail, every visit to Cloud Restaurant is a moment to remember. Every stay at CNK Hotel includes: Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi; Delicious daily breakfast; Complimentary amenities; And 24/7 front desk service — always ready to assist with warmth and care. Whether you're here for sightseeing, romance, or business, CNK Hotel offers a stylish retreat in the heart of Tbilisi – where comfort meets elegance.
